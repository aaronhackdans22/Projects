Frequently asked questions

  • What is the duration of the course?

    The duration for the course is 12 months for training and the 3 months mentorship.

  • What are the requirements to begin the course?

    University graduates and students, as well as high school graduates with demonstrable passion for technology.

  • Do you offer any scholarships?

    Yes, we offer part scholarships to needy candidates who apply to enrol in our program.

  • How much is the fees?

    The fee is GHC6000. Payment plans can be arranged for you. Part schlarships are available for applicants.

  • What is the schedule for the class?

    We have in-person weekday or weekend options for people in Accra. We also have a virtual class option for people living outside Accra, Ghana. This makes it possible for workers, students, and people spread across the globe to also participate virtually.


  • Do you require an IT or computer science

    background for Codetrain?

    No, people from diverse backgrounds of study can enrol and be successful.

Our Admission Process and Requirements

Who is the program for?

You don't need to have a background in IT to join our program. However, you are required to pass our admissions interview to make sure you are a good fit for the program.

You could be a great fit if

  • you are straight out of high school
  • you are a tertiary student
  • you are a worker
  • you come from a diverse background such as Business, Arts, Engineering, Design, etc.

Our Admission process

  1. Fill our online enrollment form
  2. Our team will give you a call to find out if you're a good fit
  3. Purchase our admission form
  4. Pay fees and start onboarding process
  5. Class starts on scheduled date
